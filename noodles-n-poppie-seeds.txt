Ingredients:

1 full pack of noodles
5 spoons of poppie seeds
2 spoons of white sugar
1 teaspoon of olive oil

Directions:

1. boil noodles until cooked
2. mix and crush poppie seeds and sugar together until forms a light power
3. coat noodles very lightly with olive oil
4. sprinkle sugar and poppie mix on noodle and mix as desired
